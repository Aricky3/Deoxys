Features

Interactive Virtual Reality Mirror Therapy

Unveil an immersive rehabilitation experience with our innovative VR software, designed to mirror real-time therapy sessions. With the power of VR, users can control a digital version of their affected limb, stimulating neural pathways to enhance recovery. It's a groundbreaking feature for patients seeking advanced rehabilitation solutions.

Personalized Therapeutic Exercises

Our VR software is not one-size-fits-all. It allows users to tailor their therapeutic exercises, considering their comfort, recovery stage, and specific needs. The adaptive VR environment modifies the virtual tasks to match individual requirements, providing a personalized therapy regimen for each user, regardless of their condition or recovery stage.

Real-time Progress Tracking and Analytics

Equipped with real-time progress tracking, our software provides users with insightful feedback on their recovery journey. Using smart algorithms, it records and analyses each therapy session, delivering comprehensive data on user improvement. This aids both therapists and users in understanding the recovery trajectory, enabling effective, data-driven modifications for future sessions.
